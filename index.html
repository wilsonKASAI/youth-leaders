About Us

Egles Young Leaders Initiative is dedicated to equipping young leaders at the secondary school level with the skills and mindset needed to become transformative leaders today and in the future. Through workshops, mentorship, and collaborative programs, we aim to inspire and empower the next generation of change-makers.
